Competition

The 1st Henryk Melcer International Piano Competition aims to promote the work of this outstanding composer from Kalisz, whose work has been forgotten and is currently not known to a wider group of music lovers. The aim of the competition is also to discover young talents as well as to promote older pianists. By participating in the competition, pianists from all over the world will have the opportunity to present their abilities to a wider audience and make their debut on the Europe’s stages. The competition is also a great platform to evaluate pianist’s current abilities and further develop piano playing skills. Therefor selected participants will be able to take part in online masterclasses conducted by members of the jury. The contestants will be judged by an international jury. In addition to the cash prizes, the finalists will have the opportunity to perform at concerts in prestigious venues in Poland, Germany and Austria.
